Pip-Boy

“Your Personal Computer”

“The most important invention of the century, the RobCo Pip-Boy has not only revolutionized portable wrist-mounted computers but it has fundamentally changed how we interact with our world.” – Unknown (most likely a RobCo employee)

The RobCo Pip-Boy (Personal Information Processor) is one of the most iconic pieces of equipment from the Fallout series. The Pip-Boy displays various pieces of information about the player such as their personal stats, inventory, a world map, a notes/quest list, and a radio. Obviously, the player finds this as an invaluable part of their arsenal during their trip across the Wasteland.

In order to advertise the various aspects of RobCo as well as give the information displayed on the Pip-Boy some flair, each Pip-Boy comes equipped with a graphical user interface mascot named Vault Boy. Vault Boy is the mascot that has become the face of the Fallout games and has become quite popular with fans of the series due to his cute and friendly appearance while also being depicted in some of the skills and traits in “inappropriate” situations.

While the Vault Boy illustrations have historically been static, with the introduction of Fallout 4 all of the Vault Boy based illustrations are animated which adds some new charm to the already iconic mascot.
