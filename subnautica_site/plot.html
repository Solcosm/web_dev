PLOT

An Ever Expanding Lore

The plot of Subnautica is evolving and changing with each new update and therefore does not have an official finalized canon. However, the current plot of the game can be summarized.

Subnautica is set in the late 22nd century when humanity began to colonize alien planets in outer space. The Alterra Corporation sent the Capital-class starship Aurora (referred to as The Aurora) to Aradne Arm to construct a phase gate. During the starships' gravitational slingshot around Planet 4546b, a seemingly unknown energy pulse causes the ship to crash-land into the planet's ocean. During the crash-landing sequence the ship expels multiple escape pods, one of which houses the player.

This is where the game begins.

After approximately three in-game days, the Aurora starship explodes, which is later found to be caused by instability of one of the internal drive cores, and releases massive amounts of radiation into the surrounding vicinity. This explosion creates openings throughout the hull of the ship for the player to explore, so long as they can survive the immense amounts of radiation. After repairing the damaged reactors and activating the various data terminals around the ship, the player learns about a previously unknown secret second mission of finding the Degasi which is a lost ship that may still have survivors on board.

After extensive exploration, the player receives a radio message detailing the passing cargo ship called the Sunbeam who received the players distress signal and offers to land on a nearby island to rescue them. Unfortunately, the Sunbeam is destroyed by an unknown canon blast as it lands.

Later, the player uncovers seemingly ancient remains of an alien race known as the Precursors, who placed Planet 4546b under quarantine after an outbreak of a disease (unknown if it is local to 4546b alone) called Carar, which apparently has spread throughout the entire planet. Defensive measures taken by the Precursors to quarantine the planet include the usage of the canon that shot down the Aurora, Degasi, and Sunbeam vessels alongside the creation of cybernetic creatures known as Warpers. The player is then shown to be infected with Carar and must search for a cure, lest it end their lives.
