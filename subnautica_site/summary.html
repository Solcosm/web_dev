SUMMARY

A Brief Rundown of the Game

Subnautica is an open world survival game that is set on the fictional planet "Planet 4546b" in which most the surface area is submerged in water. Gameplay consists of the player exploring the aquatic alien planet by scuba diving and traversing in submersible vehicles. During exploration players must gather resources and craft supplies to keep their hunger and thirst in check, while also crafting components to aid in exploration and combat. Due to the games inherent Early Access nature, certain elements are not fully implemented and thus the final product may differ from current builds.
